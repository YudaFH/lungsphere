    {% if result.label_id %}
  • Lower respiratory infection or pneumonia, especially when accompanied by cough, fever, chest pain, rapid breathing, or difficulty breathing.
  • Asthma, COPD, or chronic bronchitis, especially when wheezing, recurrent cough, excess mucus, or shortness of breath during activity is present.
  • Airway irritation or exacerbation that requires confirmation through clinical examination.
